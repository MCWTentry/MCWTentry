Fashion

Women

In the 1960s, geometric dresses were in style. For evening wear, full-skirted evening gowns were worn. In the 1960's, women began to wear trousers and other formerly masculine clothing. This was their way of demanding equal rights for women; as they are the same in the end. In 2020, the main points are pastel colors, bold colors, and a monochromatic look. Skirts are also in season.



Men

In the 1960s, typical outfits included polo shirts, khaki chino pants, striped T-shirts, and Madras plaid shirts. Now, a wardrobe can be composed of pure white, floral, and vertical stripes.



Hippie Subculture

The Hippie Subculture was an important part of the '60s. Some popular outfits of this style include, ponchos, moccasins, peace signs, medallion necklaces, polka dot-printed fabrics, and long, puffed "bubble" sleeves. The hippie subculture was a subculture that came from many parts of the world. The hippie subculture does not exist in the present day.
